Section 1 — Fundamentals of Acquihiring

Definition: Acquihiring is the acquisition of a company primarily for its people, not its product or revenue.

1.2 Motivations

  • Compress 12–18 months of team-building into 30–60 days.
  • Lock in scarce skill clusters (AI, fintech, security).
  • Preserve team cohesion that’s hard to replicate.

Section 2 — Indian Context: Ecosystem, Law, ESOPs

2.1 Why India matters

High startup density + GCC growth = constant demand for cohesive skill pods. Acquihiring offers “soft landings.”

2.2 Legal envelopes

  • Share Purchase: acquire shares, keep team.
  • Asset Purchase: acquire assets, make fresh offers.
  • Employment Transfer: resign + join with continuity.

2.3 ESOP treatments

  • Cash-out vested options.
  • Cancel-and-regrant under new plan.
  • Hybrid re-vesting models.

If you are a Recruiter

  • Work with legal on ESOP mapping + IP.
  • Prepare FAQs for candidates.
